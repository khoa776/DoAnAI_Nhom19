## Giai Thich Nhanh

- `main.py`: file chay chuong trinh.
- `config.py`: cau hinh kich thuoc cua so, tile, mau sac.
- `data/maps.py`: ma tran map, vi tri bat dau, ham kiem tra o di duoc.
- `screens/manual_map.py`: man hinh hien tai, ve map, robot, panel dieu khien.
- `ui/drawing.py`: ham ve chu, can giua chu, xu ly xuong dong.
- `algorithms/`: de san cho code thuat toan sau nay.
- `docs/`: tai lieu tom tat de mo len giai thich voi giang vien.
